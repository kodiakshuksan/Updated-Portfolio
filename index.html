About Me

I am originally from Montana. I have spent most my life growing up and attending school in Washington state. Currently I split my time between Tacoma and Bellingham. Over the past several years I have owned small businesses which include my own products, crafts, and dog grooming business. Over the past two years I have spent most my time combining my businesses into one in Tacoma. I currently own a therapy garden and non-profit gift shop that helps subsidize safe/sober housing for single/independent women. During my free time I love to play frisbee with my dog Wild. I am currently pursuing my education in Full Stack Web Development at the University of Washington.
